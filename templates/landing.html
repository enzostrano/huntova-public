Huntova

The lead-gen agent that actually finds clients.

Hunts the live web, scores prospects with your AI key, deep-researches the top tier, and sends personalised cold email locally, on your machine. No subscription. No credits. No middleware ever sees your data.

$ curl -fsSL https://huntova.com/install.sh | sh

or via pipx — pipx install huntova — then huntova

What it does

Find prospects. Write the email. Send it. Watch for replies.

All on your machine, against your AI key, your SMTP, your IMAP. The same loop every B2B SaaS sells you in pieces — collapsed into nine composable terminal commands.

🔍
Live web hunt
SearXNG-driven across Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo. 50–80 AI-generated queries per hunt, scored on five dimensions with verbatim evidence.
huntova hunt
🎯
Deep research
Crawls 14 pages of the prospect's site. Drafts an opener that references something specific they'd recognise.
huntova research
📤
Send + cadence
Step 1 opener, Day +4 bump, Day +9 final. Threads on the original subject. Auto-paused on reply.
huntova outreach send
📥
Reply detection
IMAP poll matches incoming replies to outbound message-IDs. AI classifies each one (interested / OOO / not now / wrong-person).
huntova inbox watch
📨
Deliverability
SPF / DKIM / DMARC / MX pre-flight before you mass-send. Catches the 90% of "why am I in spam" causes.
huntova doctor --email
📊
Pulse + autopilot
Weekly self-coaching summary. launchd / systemd / cron snippet runs the whole stack daily. Walk away.
huntova schedule print

Autonomous daily

Set it once. Walk away.

The whole stack runs itself once you wire the daily cron. Sequences fire. Replies land. Your morning pulse hits the log.

# wire the daily worker (macOS)
$ huntova schedule print --target launchd --at 09:00 \
    > ~/Library/LaunchAgents/com.huntova.daily.plist
$ launchctl load ~/Library/LaunchAgents/com.huntova.daily.plist

# every morning at 09:00:
#   huntova update --check     ← banner if a new release is out
#   huntova sequence run       ← fires Day +4 / +9 follow-ups
#   huntova inbox check        ← polls IMAP, classifies replies
#   huntova pulse --since 1d   ← yesterday's KPIs into the daily log

What stays on your machine

All of it.

🔑
Your AI key
OS keychain (macOS / Windows Cred Manager / libsecret), encrypted-file fallback. Never transits a Huntova server.
💾
Your leads
~/.local/share/huntova/db.sqlite — never uploaded, never synced.
📧
Your SMTP / IMAP
Your relay, your From address, your reputation. Huntova never sends through a shared sender pool.
📡
Telemetry
Off by default. huntova telemetry enable opts in if you want to help.
