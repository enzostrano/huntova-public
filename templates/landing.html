FAQ

What does 1 credit get me?

One qualified lead in your CRM: five-signal score with confidence badge, source-cited evidence quote, company details, contact name and email, and four drafted emails (opener plus three follow-ups). Failed research attempts don't cost credits.

How does Huntova find leads?

It generates targeted search queries from your Agent DNA, visits up to eight pages per prospect, scores across five signals with evidence verification, pulls contact details, and drafts personalised outreach. Live web research — not a static database.

Do I need API keys or technical setup?

No. Sign in with Google or email, spend two minutes on the wizard, and click start. No API keys, no code, no configuration.

Is Huntova GDPR compliant?

Yes. Right-to-erasure endpoint, robots.txt compliance, full audit trail, per-user data isolation. Built for European B2B from day one.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel from your account page — unused top-up credits never expire.
