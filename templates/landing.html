Huntova

An open-source lead-gen agent that runs on your machine.

Hunts the live web, scores prospects with your AI key, deep-researches the top tier, and drafts personalised cold email — locally, on your machine. Open source under AGPL-3.0. No subscription. No credits. Telemetry off by default.

What it does

Find prospects. Write the email. Send it. Watch for replies.

Every step of the outbound loop, collapsed into nine composable terminal commands. Same loop every B2B SaaS sells you in pieces — but on your machine, against your AI key, your SMTP, your IMAP.

🔭

Built different

What stays on your machine.

Local-first isn't a marketing word — it's the architecture. Hunts run on your machine; leads land in your local SQLite. Telemetry is off by default. The only time anything leaves your laptop is if you opt in to a public share link (huntova share mint) — and even then we strip personal contact data before publishing.
